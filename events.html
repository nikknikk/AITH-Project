Ace in the Hole Multisport Events is proud to offer running and triathlon events to athletes of all shapes and sizes, national origins, gender identifications and cultural backgrounds.

Multisport Weekend Events and Course Details

WATER TEMPERATURE--Expected to be between 62-66 degrees Fahrenheit. The temperature will be taken on Friday and the morning of the race. Wetsuits are recommended.



Saturday Events

Long Course Triathlon 7:00 AM

LONG COURSE SWIM--1.2 miles-- Participants will make two counter-clockwise loops. Large buoys will mark the turn points. Kayakers will be postioned on the wather to support the swimmers. Medical support will be present on the beach.

LONG COURSE BIKE--58 miles-- A scenic point-to-point course that travels over gently rolling hills prior to 3 hard climbs. The bike course will be marked with large directional signage and there will be course marshals at key intersections to help direct you.

LONG COURSE RUN--13.1 miles-- A mostly flat loop course on widely paved bike paths that traverse through and around this beautiful and scenic destination resort (2 hills total with a minimal elevation gain).




Olympic Triathlon 7:30 AM

OLYMPIC SWIM--1500 meters-- Participants will make 2 counter-clockwise loops. Large buoys will mark turn points. Kayakers will be positioned on the water to support the swimmers. Medical support will be present on the beach.

OLYMPIC BIKE--28 miles-- A scenic point-to-point course that travels over gently rolling hills. The bike course will be marked with large directional signage and course marshals will be present at key intersections.

OLYMPIC RUN--10K-- A mostly flat loop course on widely paved bike paths that traverse through and around this beautiful and scenic destination resort (1 hill total with a minimal elevation gain).




10K 7:15 AM

10k COURSE-- The 10K event starts and finishes in the Athletes Village. The paths are approximately 6 ft wide, perfectly paved and wind around through the forest. Each course has only two small hills with a minimal elevation gain to navigate and a fast downhill to flat finish to the roaring cheers of the crowd.




Half-Marathon 7:15 AM

HALF-MARATHON--13.1-miles-- Half-Marathon event starts and finishes in the Athletes Village to the cheers of the enthusiastic crowd. Once finished, runners can enjoy the finish line festivities, including the Sports & Fitness Expo and live entertainment. Post-race refreshments will be provided and the Awards Ceremony for the Half-Marathon will begin once the results have been certified.




Sunday Events

Sprint Triathlon 8:00 AM

SPRINT-- Course will offer a 1-loop 1/2 mile swim, the exact same 28 mile Bike Course as the Olympic distance and a 5km run.




Try-A-Tri 8:20 AM

TRY-A-TRI-- This novice race is designed for the first time triathlete, those new to the sport, our Junior Triathletes. The swim is a shorter, more manageable 1/4 mile distance, (instead of the standard 1/2-mile Sprint distance swim), 10 mile bike ride (vs 12 miles and it's a 2 loop course making it very spectator friendly!) and a flat 2 mile run (vs 3 mile sprint course).




Splash 'n' Dash 12:00 PM

SPLASH 'N' DASH-- Kids event for participants ages 4 to 15. This is a 100 yard swim and a 50 yard dash. It is free if an adult registers for an event otherwise it is $25.





AFTER PARTY

Enjoy time with fellow athletes, family, friends, and volunteers at our after race events!

The After Party begins Sunday the 29th at 2:00 PM following the award ceremony when the electric bluegrass band, The Legends of Oaf, will entertain participants and observers with songs from their forthcoming album, Another Anonymous November.

A selection of beer and wine will be available to observers 21 years of age and older with valid ID. Athletes 21 and older may enjoy beer and wine selections after they complete all events in which they are participating throughout the day.

Non-alcoholic beverages, such as fair trade organic coffee and tea from Caffeination Proliferation Roasters, as well as water, will also be available. Healthy food and snacks will be available for purchase--including vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and pescatarian options.
