EVENTS FOR EVERY BODY

Ace in the Hole Multisport Events is proud to offer running and triathlon events to athletes of all shapes and sizes, national origins, gender identifications and cultural backgrounds.

Ace in the Hole Multisport Weekend

Our Annual Ace in the Hole Multisport Weekend is a legendary event in Oregon triathlon and running communities. It has become a traditional destination race for athletes from across the nation.
We offer something for every level of athletic ability. The weekend includes a first timer triathlon, a sprint, Olympic and Half-Iron triathlons, and 10K and Half-Marathon runs. Come to experience your first race or come to compete to win, but make sure you come to have fun!
